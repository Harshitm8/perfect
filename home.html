About Us

About Indo Thai Global

At Indo Thai Global, we deliver advanced solar and hybrid drying solutions designed to empower farmers, food processors and industries with sustainable and cost-effective technology. Our systems harness the natural power of the sun to ensure hygienic, uniform and energy efficient drying for a wide range of agricultural products.

INDO THAI GLOBAL combines innovation with practicality, offering high-performance solar dryers that improve product quality, extend shelf life and reduce post-harvest losses. With a focus on durability, efficiency and eco-friendly practices, we provide customized solutions tailored to your specific needs. From small-scale farms to large industrial operations, we are committed to driving a cleaner, smarter and more sustainable future.

Mission
Our Mission

Empowering Farmers & Processors with Clean Energy

Our mission is to deliver reliable, affordable and eco-friendly solar drying solutions that help farmers, food processors and agri-businesses reduce post-harvest losses, cut energy costs and improve product quality — all without harming the environment.

  • Make solar drying accessible to small and large-scale agri-businesses
  • Reduce post-harvest losses across agriculture, fisheries and herbal sectors
  • Replace conventional fuel-based drying with zero-emission solar technology
  • Deliver consistent, food-safe and export-grade output for every customer
Our Vision

A Sustainable Future Powered by the Sun

We envision a world where every farm, processing unit and cooperative harnesses the power of solar energy for drying — eliminating dependency on fossil fuels, reducing carbon footprint and building a stronger, greener agri-food ecosystem across India and beyond.

  • Become India's most trusted solar dryer manufacturer by 2030
  • Expand solar drying adoption to 10,000+ farms and food units
  • Pioneer hybrid and smart solar drying technologies
  • Support sustainable farming and food security at a national scale
