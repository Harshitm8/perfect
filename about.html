Our Story

Who We Are

Indo Thai Global is focused on delivering reliable solar drying solutions and food processing equipment designed to support modern agriculture and sustainable energy practices across India.

Since our founding in 2005, we have built a reputation for manufacturing durable, efficient and easy-to-operate equipment that helps farmers and food processors reduce post-harvest losses and improve productivity.

What Drives Us

We believe that sustainable technology should be accessible to all scales of operation — from small family farms to large commercial food processing facilities.

Every product we build reflects our commitment to quality materials, practical engineering and long-term value for our customers.
